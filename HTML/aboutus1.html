About Us

We are just a group of friends trying to make our teacher's life easier.

We are students belonging to Indian Institute of Information Technology, Lucknow, who've come together to build this Attendance taking website to make it easier for our teachers to take the attendance of the students. Not only will this only take a fews seconds instead of 10-20 minutes, but it will also avoid the chance of there being any false attendance being registered (NO PROXY).
